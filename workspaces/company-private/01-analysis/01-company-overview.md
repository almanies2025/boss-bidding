# MBC Group — Company Overview

## Executive Summary

MBC Group (Middle East Broadcasting Center) is the largest free-to-air broadcaster in the Arab world and operates the leading Arabic streaming platform (Shahid/MBC Shahid). Founded in London in 1991, it is now headquartered in Riyadh and is majority-owned by Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF). As of FY 2025, the group generates SAR 5.4 billion (~$1.44 billion) in annual revenue across three business segments: Broadcasting & Other Commercial Activities (BOCA), MBC Shahid (streaming), and Media & Entertainment (production/events).

## Key Subsidiaries and Business Lines

### MBC Shahid (Streaming)
- Launched 2011 as AVOD; relaunched 2019 with SVOD (Shahid+)
- Rebranded to MBC Shahid in 2026
- ~5 million SVOD subscribers (FY 2024); ~6 million estimated end-2025
- 18.6 million monthly active AVOD users
- Library: 9,000+ titles; #1 Arabic streaming platform
- 2025 revenue: SAR 1.38 billion (+28% YoY)
- Two landmark bundle deals in 2025: Netflix (July) and Disney+/OSN+ (December)

### Al Arabiya News Network
- 24-hour Arabic news channel, headquartered Riyadh (relocated from Dubai, completed July 2025)
- Estimated ~$93.6 million annual revenue (own reports, 2025)
- MBC Media Solutions manages advertising sales for Al Arabiya Network
- Competitors: Al Jazeera (Qatar-state), Sky News Arabia, France 24

### MBC Studios
- Established 2018; now with ~100 productions in pipeline
- Notable productions: "Rashash," "Rise of the Witches," "HWJN," "Desert Warrior" (with Anthony Mackie)
- Co-productions with Hollywood studios
- Premiered "The Voice of Hind Rajab" at Venice International Film Festival
- Target: Saudi and pan-Arab audiences; international coproduction growing

### MBC Media Solutions
- Commercial and ad sales arm of the group
- Exclusive advertising sales partnership with Al Arabiya News Network
- ~45% share of TV advertising market in MENA claimed by group

### MBC Academy
- Media training institution for regional broadcasting professionals

### MBC Talent Agency
- Represents on-screen talent for MBC productions and channels

### MBC Ventures
- Investment arm; participates in tech-media partnerships and startups

- **Registered office**: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (relocated from Dubai 2022)
- **Al Arabiya**: Riyadh (relocated July 2025)

- **Audiences reached**: 165+ million viewers weekly across MENA; international Arabic diaspora in Europe, North America, Australia
- **Reach**: 22 Arab countries, with Persian-language reach into Iran
